Coraline

Cost 20$

Author Neil Gaiman

Written in 2002

Coraline is a story about a girl who discovers another apartment outside the door of her new home, like two drops of water similar to her own. Other mom and dad live there, who want Coraline to stay with them. The girl will have to be very brave to return to her real home and find herself.
